# 国家密码管理局 — 科普园地

## Core Content

Official public cryptography education from China's State Cryptography Administration (OSCCA).

### Article Series

**密码的奥秘 (The Mystery of Passwords/Cryptography)** — 6 parts:
1. Origins of cryptography
2. Classical cryptography
3. Mechanical encryption
4. Modern cryptography
5. Cryptanalysis
6. Future trends

**密码趣谈 (Interesting Talks on Cryptography)** — 8 parts:
1. Symmetric encryption
2. Asymmetric encryption
3. Historical Chinese cryptographic methods
4. Cryptography and civilization

### Note
This is public-facing educational content, not technical standards documentation.
